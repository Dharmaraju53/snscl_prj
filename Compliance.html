Compliance

Committed to excellence in responsibility, ethics, and sustainability.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

We actively support Saudization by hiring and training local talent, offering incentives, and empowering Saudi women. SNSCL also collaborates with colleges to offer internship opportunities for students across the Kingdom.

Sustainability

Our sustainability practices focus on minimizing waste, maximizing recyclable content, reducing single-use materials, and promoting awareness among our employees on environmentally responsible behavior.

Health, Safety & Environment (HSE)

SNSCL prioritizes employee safety through rigorous HSE compliance audits and the implementation of a dedicated Health Safety Environment (HSE) manual across all operations.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Our performance is continuously measured through KPIs like customer satisfaction, internal process efficiency, employee satisfaction, and financial performance index.

Quality Control

We deliver quality across all activities with a robust Quality Management System (QMS) aligned to ISO9001 standards, ensuring we meet obligations to both customers and stakeholders.

Ethics

Integrity is central to SNSCL. We uphold values of honesty, accountability, and respect, ensuring all materials are sourced from ethically compliant suppliers and monitored under our corporate Code of Conduct.
