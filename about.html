Our Story

With over 35 years of experience in delivering highly complex, mission-critical projects, Saudi Naval Support Company (SNSCL) has become a trusted name in defense and commercial sectors across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

We operate with the highest standards of quality, safety, and technical excellence — integrating seamlessly with clients and local supply chains. Our expertise spans construction, operations & maintenance, facility management, logistics, and telecommunications.

Over the decades, SNSCL has grown into one of the most diversified support service providers in the Kingdom, fulfilling national and public missions while enabling our clients to achieve operational excellence.

Today, SNSCL leads in large-scale projects both locally and internationally. Our success is driven by client satisfaction and our ability to adapt to a wide spectrum of technical and engineering challenges.

Governance Structure

Shareholder Empowerment

The structure begins with General Meetings of Shareholders, including both Annual and Extraordinary General Meetings. Shareholders play a key role in electing the Board of Directors and shaping company direction.

Transparent Leadership Oversight

The Board of Directors, elected by shareholders, ensures strategic oversight and long-term value creation. The Chairman leads the board and oversees all committee activities.

Commitment to Ethical Standards

An active Ethics Committee reinforces the company’s dedication to ethical business conduct and compliance.
